Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

UNIT collects the following information when you register as a resident: name, address, unit number, phone number, and delivery preferences. Drivers provide name, phone number, and company affiliation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send delivery notifications via SMS. These include pre-route alerts and proximity notifications when your driver is within 0.5 miles of your address. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. SMS Communications

By submitting your phone number through the UNIT resident portal, you expressly consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your deliveries. Message frequency varies based on delivery activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely and used only for delivery coordination. We recommend using hints rather than full access codes in your delivery notes (e.g. "last 4 of address" instead of the full code). We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting support@unitdelivery.app.

4a. Driver Location Data

Driver GPS coordinates are transmitted during active deliveries only and are used solely to display the driver's location on the customer tracking screen. Driver location data is not permanently stored, sold, or shared with any third party beyond the active delivery session.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@unitdelivery.app. You may opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.

6. Third-Party Services

UNIT uses Twilio to deliver SMS notifications. Twilio's privacy policy applies to message transmission. We do not share personal data with Twilio beyond what is necessary to send your delivery notifications.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@unitdelivery.app
