Step 1 of 8
How to Import Your Route
  1. Open the Speed X app and go to your route for today.
  2. Screenshot or copy your stop list.
  3. In UNIT tap "New Route" at the bottom nav.
  4. Paste or upload your route — UNIT reads the addresses automatically.
  5. Tap Import and your stops load in order.
💡 UNIT auto-fills phone numbers and drop spots for addresses already in the system.
🗑️
Step 2 of 8
How to Delete a Route
  1. Go to your Home screen.
  2. Find your active route at the top.
  3. Tap the red Clear button in the top-right of the route card.
  4. Confirm the prompt — all stops are removed.
⚠️ Only do this when starting a new day or if you imported the wrong file.
💳
Step 3 of 8
How to Cancel Your Subscription
  1. Tap Account in the bottom nav.
  2. Tap Manage Billing & Cancel.
  3. This opens your Stripe billing portal.
  4. Tap Cancel Plan and confirm.
💡 Your account stays active until the end of your billing period after cancelling.
🔗
Step 4 of 8
How to Send the Tracking Link Manually
If a customer didn't get an SMS or wants to track live:
  1. Open an active stop.
  2. Tap "Send Link" — your phone's texting app opens with the link pre-filled.
  3. Hit send. Customer gets a live map of your location.
💡 Uses your personal number — best backup when automated SMS isn't working.
📍
Step 5 of 8
How to Turn On Location in Safari
UNIT needs your location for GPS tracking and proximity alerts.

On iPhone:
  1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  2. Scroll down, tap Safari Websites.
  3. Select "While Using the App".
  4. Return to UNIT and refresh.
💡 If UNIT is on your home screen, allow location when prompted on launch.
📱
Step 6 of 8
Add UNIT to Your Home Screen
Run UNIT like a native app — no App Store needed.

On iPhone (must use Safari):
  1. Open UNIT in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share icon (box with arrow up) at the bottom.
  3. Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add — UNIT appears on your home screen.
💡 Full-screen mode + faster load when opened from home screen.
📌
Step 7 of 8
How to Move a Pin & Fix the Geofence
If the geofence alert isn't triggering or the map pin is wrong:
  1. Open the stop (tap Active or the address).
  2. Tap "Fix Pin" on the map.
  3. Drag the pin to the exact delivery spot.
  4. Tap Save Location.
The fix is saved permanently — all future drivers at that address use your correction automatically.
💡 Geofence radius is ~150 ft. An off pin = alert fires too early or not at all.
📞
Step 8 of 8
How to Change Your Phone Number
  1. Tap Account in the bottom nav.
  2. Tap Edit Profile.
  3. Update your number and tap Save.
Your new number is used for all future SMS alerts and the manual tracking link.
💡 Keep this accurate — customers receive texts from this number when you use manual send.
