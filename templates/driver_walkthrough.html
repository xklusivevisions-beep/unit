Step 1 of 8
How to Import Your Route
  1. Open the Speed X app and go to your route for today.
  2. Screenshot or copy your stop list.
  3. In UNIT, tap "New Route" at the bottom.
  4. Paste or upload your route — UNIT reads the addresses automatically.
  5. Tap Import and your stops will load in order.
💡 UNIT auto-fills phone numbers and drop spots for addresses already in the system.
🗑️
Step 2 of 8
How to Delete a Route
  1. Go to your Home screen (the dashboard).
  2. Find your active route at the top.
  3. Tap the red Clear button in the top-right corner of the route.
  4. Confirm the prompt — all stops will be removed.
⚠️ This deletes all stops for that route. Only do this when you're starting a new day or imported the wrong file.
💳
Step 3 of 8
How to Cancel Your Subscription
  1. Tap Account in the bottom nav.
  2. Scroll down to Billing.
  3. Tap Manage Subscription — this opens your Stripe billing portal.
  4. Tap Cancel Plan and confirm.
💡 Your account stays active until the end of your billing period after cancelling.
🔗
Step 4 of 8
How to Send the Tracking Link Manually
If a customer didn't get an SMS or wants to track you manually:
  1. Open a stop by tapping Active or the stop address.
  2. Tap "Send Link" — this opens your phone's default texting app with the link pre-filled.
  3. Hit send. The customer gets a live map showing your location.
💡 This uses your personal number — great backup when automated SMS isn't working.
📍
Step 5 of 8
How to Turn On Location in Safari
UNIT needs your location to track your route and trigger proximity alerts.

On iPhone:
  1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  2. Scroll down and tap Safari Websites.
  3. Set to "While Using the App".
  4. Come back to UNIT and refresh the page.
💡 If UNIT is added to your home screen, location permissions may be separate — allow when prompted on launch.
📱
Step 6 of 8
How to Add UNIT to Your Home Screen
Run UNIT like a native app — no App Store needed.

On iPhone (Safari):
  1. Open UNIT in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share icon (box with arrow) at the bottom.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add — UNIT appears on your home screen like an app.
💡 Opening from your home screen gives you full-screen mode and faster load times.
📌
Step 7 of 8
How to Move a Pin (Fix Wrong Location)
If the geofence isn't triggering or the map pin is in the wrong spot:
  1. Tap the stop to open it (Active view).
  2. Tap "Fix Pin" on the map.
  3. Drag the pin to the correct delivery location.
  4. Tap Save Location.
The corrected pin is saved permanently — every driver going to that address will use your correction automatically.
💡 The geofence radius is ~150 ft. If the pin is off, the proximity alert may fire too early or not at all.
📞
Step 8 of 8
How to Change Your Phone Number
  1. Tap Account in the bottom nav.
  2. Tap Edit Profile.
  3. Update your phone number and tap Save.
Your new number will be used for all future SMS delivery alerts and your manual tracking link.
💡 Make sure your number is correct — customers receive texts from this number when you use the manual send option.
